The Workspace

Well, 2020 has been a not-so-nice year so far, what with the global pandemic and everything..
Corporate jobs, especially software development have been pushed to change their work culture, with many companies opting for 50%-100% remote work.

So, what does a truly inspiring and efficient workspace consist of?

Here's my attempt at describing a 3D rendition of an ideal workspace for maximum productivity.
Built using three.js with webpack, you can explore and interact with the essential tools that make up the work-from-home setup to get a feel of what its like, being an efficient employee in pyjamas!

Works best in Google Chrome
Please use earphones for the full experience
